Advertise or partner

Math and Physics Practice Lab is open to selective partnerships that are useful to students, tutors, and teachers. This page is the right place for sponsorship, affiliate, or collaboration inquiries related to education.

What kind of partnerships fit best

  • Relevant tutoring, textbook, or education-tool sponsorships.
  • Clearly disclosed affiliate recommendations tied to real student needs.
  • Course-relevant newsletter, worksheet, or resource collaborations.
  • Carefully selected placements that do not hurt the learning experience.

Partnership principles

  • Student usefulness comes before volume of ads or promotions.
  • Disclosures should remain clear and easy to find.
  • Only education-relevant offers should appear beside course content.
  • Privacy and trust pages should stay accessible at all times.

Useful links

If you want to discuss a relevant collaboration, use the contact page and include the educational context of your proposal.
